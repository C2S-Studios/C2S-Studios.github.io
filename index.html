C2S PassMan

Self-hosted, open source password manager suite, where you control access and management of your sensitive data, we just give you something to start with, but the rest is up to you.

Let's begin

Self-Hosted

Your data should never be managed by someone else, let alone big corporations. With C2S PassMan, you're in control again; we give you the building blocks, you decide on how to set them up.

Innovative

Powered by latest and greatest trends in software development, the entire suite is developed with modern technologies, from backend and CMS, to client-side apps, we bring the best.

Open Source

Powered by open source technologies, it only makes sense to make our software free, and available for all. Freedom, transparency and collaboration are core values we stand for.

Privacy matters. We know this better than anyone.

As Europeans, we value our privacy and individual liberties. No corporation, or government, should have access to your digital data, such as personal account credentials, your notes or banking info.

Complete transparency. Freely modify and adapt our software.

Our software comes operational out of the box, but that doesn't mean you can't customize it. We're looking forward to seeing you tinker around, experiment, maybe even help us improve it. We're stronger together, that's why we went open source from the start.

Simple installation. It's all on GitLab.

To get you up and running, we have prepared extensive documentation; initial configuration is as simple as running few bash scripts, while well-structured and documented codebase allows for further exploration.
